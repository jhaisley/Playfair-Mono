Playfair Mono is a monospaced variant of Claus Eggers Sørensen's popular transitional serif design, Playfair. While preserving the distinctive high contrast, delicate serifs, and historic aesthetics of late 18th-century pointed steel pen typography, Playfair Mono locks all glyphs to a fixed advance width of exactly 700 units.

This monospaced version is well-suited for editorial layouts, programming, structured tabular data, and technical typesetting. It features a variable font format interpolating across Weight (Light to Black) and Optical Size (6pt to 1200pt), allowing for flexible design ranges from small-print body copy to large, display-size headlines.

To contribute, see the project repository at github.com/jhaisley/Playfair-Mono.
